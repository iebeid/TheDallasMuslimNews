Executive Summary

This directory provides a comprehensive list of Islamic organizations operating within the Dallas-Fort Worth (DFW) metroplex and an approximate 100-mile radius. The compilation includes non-profit organizations, Islamic centers and mosques, community associations, and educational institutions. The data reveals a diverse and active Muslim community with a well-developed infrastructure catering to religious, educational, social welfare, advocacy, and professional needs.
